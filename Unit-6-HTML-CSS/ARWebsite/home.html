About Amber

2025

I’m currently learning front-end web development with a focus on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. As someone naturally drawn to both creativity and structure, coding has become the perfect space to grow. I enjoy building clean, responsive designs and bringing ideas to life through the browser.

Blogging Through the Learning Process As I develop my technical skills, I document my learning experience through blogging and digital content. I believe in learning out loud — sharing challenges, wins, and useful resources that might help others starting their own journey in tech.

I’m proudly from the west side of Detroit (Brightmoor). My city has taught me the value of resilience, originality, and hustle. I bring that energy into everything I create — whether it’s a website, a video, or a new skill I’m learning.

Building a Creative Tech Presence Outside of code, I create lifestyle and tech-focused content across platforms like TikTok and YouTube. My goal is to merge creativity and technology in a way that inspires others — especially young Black women — to explore careers in STEM and digital creation. Lastly Being a dog mom has added structure to my life while I balance self-paced learning, content creation, and my 9–5. It’s helped me build better habits, stay disciplined, and commit to long-term growth — all of which supports my goals in tech.
