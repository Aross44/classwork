Portfolio

2025

Amber Ross is a multi-talented creative and aspiring developer based in Detroit, blending a passion for technology, storytelling, and self-expression. With a background in tech support and content creation, Amber is now diving into the world of software development, using her portfolio to document her hands-on learning experience. From building interactive web projects to exploring the fundamentals of front-end development, this site showcases her growth mindset, dedication to skill-building, and commitment to inspiring others—especially young Black women pursuing paths in tech and media. This portfolio serves as both a digital journal and a creative lab, where Amber experiments with code, shares insights from her journey, and brings her personality into every section. Visitors can expect a mix of personal storytelling, technical projects, and reflections that highlight her evolution as a learner and future software developer. Whether you’re a collaborator, recruiter, or fellow creative, this site offers an authentic look at what it means to build something meaningful from the ground up—one line of code at a time.
