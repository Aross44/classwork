About Me

2025

Hi, I'm Amber Ross—a Detroit native, tech enthusiast, and creative storyteller. I'm currently learning front-end development while balancing my role in tech support at Geek Squad and creating lifestyle content across platforms. My passion lies in merging creativity with technology, whether I'm building a web project, editing a video, or documenting my self-improvement journey. As a proud Black woman in tech, I aim to make space for authenticity, curiosity, and growth. I'm especially interested in inspiring others—particularly Black women and girls—to explore careers in coding, media, and digital creation. This portfolio is my way of tracking my progress, sharing what I learn, and building something real from the ground up.
